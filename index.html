Manoj Das

(27 February 1934 – 27 April 2021)

Manoj Das was a renowned Odia and English writer, recipient of Padma Shri and Padma Bhushan.

Known as the “Vishnu Sharma of Odia Literature,” he contributed to short stories, novels, poetry, essays, and children's literature.

He served as Professor of English at the Sri Aurobindo International Centre of Education.
