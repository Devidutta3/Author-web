The Proud King and the Squirrel

Once upon a time, there was a king who was very proud of his matchless position. He was young and intelligent...

The king believed he was superior to everyone. But a tiny squirrel began mocking him, chanting verses about wealth and pride. Furious, the king tried to silence the squirrel, yet failed each time. Finally, he learned the wisdom of ignoring vanity.

The Whisper

In a small village lived two boys — Raghu and Raju. One was honest and humble, the other clever but dishonest...

Years later, Raju became Prime Minister through deceit. Raghu, disguised, exposed corruption through a clever plan involving whispers and gold. The King realized truth and justice prevailed.
