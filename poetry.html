Whispers of the River

The river flows in silent grace, Through shadowed woods and open space...

The river sings of ancient times, Of forgotten dreams and sacred rhymes. Beneath the moon and silver sky, It carries secrets drifting by. Flowing gently, calm and free, It whispers truth eternally.

Evening Silence

When twilight wraps the fading day, And golden light dissolves away...

A hush descends on restless lands, Soft winds drift through silent sands. Stars awaken, one by one, As darkness greets the setting sun.
